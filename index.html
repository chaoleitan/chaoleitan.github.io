Research

I'm interested in computer vision, deep learning, generative AI, and image processing. Most of my research is about inferring the physical world (shape, motion, color, light, etc) from images, usually with radiance fields. Some papers are highlighted.

Hierarchical Semantic Correspondence Networks for Video Paragraph Grounding
Chaolei Tan, Zihang Lin, Jian-Fang Hu, Wei-Shi Zheng, Jianhuang Lai
CVPR, 2023
paper

The first hierarchical modeling framework for video paragraph grounding, which enables to align multi-level video-language semantic correspondences for decoding temporal grounding results at multiple granularities.
