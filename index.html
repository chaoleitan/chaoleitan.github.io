Chaolei Tan

I am an incoming PhD student at Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Long Chen. Previously, I obtained Master's degree from Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), supervised by Prof. Jian-Fang Hu and Prof. Wei-Shi Zheng. I received Bachelor's degree from SYSU in 2021.

At Google I've worked on Glass, Lens Blur, HDR+, VR, Portrait Mode, Portrait Light, and Maps. I did my PhD at UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Jitendra Malik. I've received the PAMI Young Researcher Award.
